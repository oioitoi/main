feeling good

As an old year rolls into a new one, sitting in the window with the warmth of sunshine reflected off the fallen snow, I’m reminded of the lyrics to Nina Simone’s hit "Feeling Good".

It's a new dawn
It's a new day
It's a new life
For me
And I'm feeling good.

So, it’s a new year… new day… new life… new intentions… new beginnings… new blog!
And I’m feeling good.

*and will feel even better once we have cleared the snow from our driveway and we can get to the supermarket!
