welcome to oioitoi

oioitoi is a woman-owned business with toys designed by a product-designer and educator... who also happens to be the mother of three wonderfully creative kids. Our website is currently under construaction, so please return later to find out more about oioitoi, and the story behind our unique toys.

our mission

Our mission is to design and deliver safe, fun, and engaging toys that foster literacy and numeracy skills while encouraging curiosity and creativity. We aim to make all toys within the USA using sustainable, locally-sourced materials.
