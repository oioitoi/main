welcome to oioitoi

oioitoi wants every child to have the foundational skills they need to thrive and succeed at school. Known as the 'readiness gap', approximately 40% to 70% of children in the US arrive at kindergarten without the necessary skills they need to make a successful start.

our mission

oioitoi's mission is to enrich early childhood learning through thoughtfully designed toys that meet children where they are. To enable learning through the vitality and joy of spontaneous and cooperative play and provide the foundations for literacy, numeracy, communication, fine and gross motor skills, and social/emotional readiness.
