our mission

At oioitoi, we specialize in creating children's toys and learning resources that focus on fun, and have a simple timeless aesthetic. Our toys encourage literacy and numeracy skills, and a natural curiosity through play. Based in the USA, we prioritize the use of safe and sustainable local materials by local artisans.
